Institutional Structure.
The institution is administered and managed by a Board Of Directors(BOD) and the Board Of Governor (BOG). The professional head of the institute is the Principal. The principal is assisted by:
  1. The deputy principal administration and management.
  2. The deputy principal finance and planning.

Others include:

  1. The academic registrar
  2. The dean of students.
  3. Heads of department.
