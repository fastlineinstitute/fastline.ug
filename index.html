OUR VISION

Fastline Institute of Higher Education-Kampala will be a world class leader in preparing professionals with tested, empirical, practical and observable knowledge, skills and values to positively change their lives and communities in this dynamic global world. This will be achieved through Establishing a state of art campus, nurturing and growing our institute, students, faculty, other staffs and communities tied to our mission, values and belief systems. In this, we shall have a transformative impact on the society through students’ and faculty’s continual innovation and growth in global educational systems, research, creativity, perfection and community engagement.

OUR MISSION

The mission of Fastline Institute of Higher Education-Kampala is a driving force for achieving holistic Excellency by actively engaging the students, faculty and other staffs to embrace diversity and inclusion; inspiring positive changes and sustaining our community. By initiating all members into Fastline family; students are prepared for global community by providing a nurturing environment that connects the student to the global world. This will be achieved by:
a) Teaching others, learning from others academically and scholarly, interacting with others, nurturing our students so as to rise and grow them into leaders of tomorrow in all spheres of life.
b) Offering dynamic, detailed, Practical and forceful instructions using effective and efficient techniques, methodologies, skills, strategies. And interactive kind of education so as to ensure forceful and effective content mastery that engages the students to be lifelong scholars. This will serve as a bridge that shall allow our students to reach their full potentials and achieve their dreams in life.
